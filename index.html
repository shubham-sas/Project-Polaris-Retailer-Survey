SECTION 1

Store Profiling

Est. 4–5 min · Observe where possible, ask when needed

Q1MEDIUMOBSERVE
What is the approximate size of this store?
Count your steps from wall to wall — 1 step ≈ 2.5 feet. Multiply length × width to get sq ft.
e.g. 10 steps × 8 steps = ~200 sq ft
Q2MEDIUMASK
What are the store's total yearly sales across ALL products?
Ask the retailer directly. This includes all categories — groceries, beverages, snacks, everything.
Q3HIGHASK
What are the store's total BEVERAGE sales in a year?
Beverages = packaged juices, drinks, water, soda, energy drinks. Do NOT include milk or tea.
Q4HIGHOBSERVE
How many Visicoolers (Fridges) are in the store?
Count all glass-door fridges in the store. Include both open and closed fridges used for beverages.
Q5MEDIUMOBSERVE
What marketing materials are visible for beverage brands?
Walk around the store. Note posters, danglers, shelf strips, fridge branding, and any other visibility materials.

SECTION 2

Category Sales & Brand Share

Est. 5–7 min · Ask retailer for all numbers

Q9HIGHASK
What are the monthly sales (₹) for each brand in each category?
Ask the retailer how much they sell per month for each brand. Write ₹ value. If they don't know exact, ask them to estimate. e.g. "I sell about 50 bottles of Paper Boat per month at ₹30 = ₹1500"
Q10LOWASK
For each category — is it growing or declining, and why?
Ask: "Compared to 2 years ago, are you selling more or less of [category]? Why do you think that is?"
Q11MEDIUMASK
Do customers prefer small packs or large packs? Has this changed?
Ask: "Do more people buy the small bottle or the big bottle? Was it different a few years ago?"
Q12HIGHASK
For each brand stocked — how many flavours and pack sizes do you carry?
Enter the number AND names of flavours stocked (e.g. 4 — Mango, Litchi, Mixed Fruit, Cranberry). Similarly enter pack sizes stocked (e.g. 200ml, 500ml). Write manually — no dropdowns.
Q13HIGHASK
How has demand for coconut water changed in this store?
Ask: "Are more customers asking for coconut water now compared to before? Which brand do they ask for by name?"
